About

J.C. Britton Publishing is a small, veteran owned editing house and press dedicated to philosophical, theological, and other creative works that might not find a home elsewhere. We specialize in literary fiction, essay collections and monograms in philosophy and theology, and experimental works. The Chicago Manual of Style (CMOS-17th or 18th edition) is our primary style guide.
